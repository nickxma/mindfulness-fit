Should You Get Into Mindfulness?

Most mindfulness content assumes the answer is yes. This tool doesn't.

Mindfulness shifts emphasis away from identity-driven narrative toward moment-to-moment perception. Depending on whether narrative is currently helping or hurting you, that shift can be useful or costly.

Six questions. No account required. About two minutes.

Part of the Paradox of Acceptance toolkit. These tools draw on a wide survey of contemplative teachers and traditions.
